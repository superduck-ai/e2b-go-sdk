# E2B Contributor License Agreement

This page summarizes the contributor agreement for E2B and points contributors to the signing flow.

In short:

- contributors keep ownership of their work
- contributors grant E2B the licenses needed to use and distribute contributions
- contributors confirm they have the legal right to submit the contribution
- contributors should disclose any third-party restrictions they know about

If you need to contribute code, documentation, or other project changes, review the CLA terms and sign it through the E2B CLA flow before submitting changes when required.
